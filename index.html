selected work · closer up

jobs.jtplatt.com

A personal distributed system that runs my job search. A daily launchd cron on my Mac crawls four public-sector job boards, scores each posting against a structured inventory of my experience in two tiers (a cheap deterministic filter, then an LLM pass on the survivors), and publishes a static report the server serves behind auth. Rate-limited, robots-aware, and built so an empty scrape never overwrites the last good run.

Solo build · Python, Flask, SQLite, BeautifulSoup, Claude API · 2026

The Sneaker Savant

Budweiser "Heroes" microsite

A community-engagement microsite for Budweiser's "Heroes" Salute at Super Bowl XLVIII, built with Folds of Honor and reaching more than 40 million people. It ran the campaign's voting and user-generated content: visitors nominated their own veterans and voted on the National Guard homecoming parade at its center. Real product behind the ad, not a static page. Built at Thismoment, whose curated content streams predated the timeline interface Facebook later made standard.

Front-end engineer · voting + UGC, HTML/CSS/JS on a proprietary CMS · Super Bowl XLVIII, 2014
